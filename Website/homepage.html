About Our Project

For this project we decieded to build an AI model with the task of early onset Alzheimer's disease detection. Our AI model will take MRI images of brain scans and determine whether or not the patient is developing early onset Alzheimer's. Based on the images given our AI model will determine whether or not our patient has early onset Alzhimer's disease.
