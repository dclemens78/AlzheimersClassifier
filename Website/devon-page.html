About Me

My name is Devon Pedraza, and I am a senior in Computer Science with a concentration on Full Stack Web Development currently attending Florida Southern College. Within these four years, I have developed skills in database management, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In this project, I took on the role of designing and implementing the frontend of our website while also contributing backend JavaScript in order to handle image uploads both for the API and our Alzheimer's Classifier AI. My goal has been to create a professional and responsive design, making sure that the platform is accessible with different screen sizes. This makes it easier for users to learn about Alzheimer's disease and utilize our classifier with MRI scans. This project really pushed me out of my comfort zone and deepened my understanding of Full Stack development, while still taking an introductory course in it. It has been a very transformational learning experience that has crystallized my passion for crafting innovative web solutions. I look forward to continuing to grow as a developer and am eager to pursue a career in Full Stack development in the tech industry.
